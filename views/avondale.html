AVONDALE, BIRMINGHAM'S HISTORIC BREWERY

There's nothing better than good stories told over great beer. That's why at Avondale Brewing Company we've named all of our beers after the folklore of Avondale, Alabama. We craft our brews with the same unique character and style as the stories they are named after. A collaboration between Coby and Hunter Lake, Avondale Brewery is a locally owned and operated brewery in the heart of the Deep South.
